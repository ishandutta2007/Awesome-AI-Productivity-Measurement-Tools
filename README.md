# Awesome-AI-Productivity-Measurement-Tools
## Top AI Productivity Measurement Tools Ecosystem

**Curated List of SaaS Products & Open-Source GitHub Projects**  
*Focused on Engineering Productivity, Team Performance & Developer Analytics*  
**Last updated: March 2026**

This repository tracks notable **SaaS platforms** and **open-source projects** building **AI Productivity Measurement Tools**. These platforms analyze code commits, pull requests, tickets, collaboration patterns, and engineering signals to provide actionable insights into team velocity, developer experience, bottlenecks, and overall productivity.

**Examples** include DX, Swarmia, Faros AI, Weave, Exceeds AI, We360.ai, and 48DX (the category leaders). Tools listed here emphasize **AI-driven insights**, objective metrics, developer experience scoring, and continuous improvement recommendations.

**Open-source emphasis**: This section is heavily expanded with every major active project for self-hosting, local data processing, full data privacy, and complete customization — ideal for engineering teams and organizations that want sovereign productivity analytics without sending sensitive data to third parties.

Contributions welcome! Open a PR to add/update entries. Keep descriptions factual and link to official sites.

## Open-Source GitHub Projects

### Dedicated Productivity & Engineering Analytics Tools

- **[Faros CE](https://github.com/faros-ai/faros-community-edition)**  
  Open-source community edition of Faros AI for engineering intelligence, data ingestion, and productivity analytics.

- **[Augur](https://github.com/chaoss/augur)**  
  Open-source platform for measuring the health and productivity of open-source communities and internal engineering teams.

- **[GrimoireLab](https://github.com/chaoss/grimoirelab)**  
  Powerful open-source toolkit for software development analytics, including productivity metrics and visualizations.

- **[OpenTelemetry + Custom Dashboards](https://github.com/open-telemetry)**  
  Industry-standard observability framework widely used to build custom engineering productivity measurement systems.

- **[Metrics](https://github.com/chaoss/metrics)** (CHAOSS Project)  
  Community-driven metrics models and implementations for measuring open-source and internal team productivity.

- **[GitLab Analytics (Self-Hosted)](https://github.com/gitlabhq/gitlabhq)**  
  Full open-source DevOps platform with built-in productivity analytics, value stream mapping, and DORA metrics.

- **[Prometheus + Grafana Engineering Dashboards](https://github.com/prometheus/prometheus)**  
  Popular open-source stack for building real-time engineering productivity and velocity dashboards.

- **[LinearB Open Alternatives](https://github.com/search?q=engineering+productivity+analytics)**  
  Multiple community-driven open-source engineering intelligence tools and dashboards.

- **[DevOps Metrics Tools](https://github.com/search?q=dora+metrics+open+source)**  
  Various open-source implementations of DORA metrics and SPACE framework measurements.

### Additional Strong Open-Source Options

- **[CHAOSS](https://github.com/chaoss)** — Entire ecosystem of productivity and community health metrics.
- **[All Contributors](https://github.com/all-contributors/all-contributors)** — Recognition and contribution analytics.
- **[GitHub CLI + Custom Scripts](https://github.com/cli/cli)** — For building personal/team productivity measurement tools.
- **LangGraph + CrewAI** templates for AI-powered productivity analysis agents.
- **n8n / Huginn** workflows for automated engineering metrics collection and insights.

**Frameworks for building custom solutions**: Combine **Faros CE**, **GrimoireLab**, **Augur**, and **OpenTelemetry** with **Grafana** and local LLMs (Ollama) to create fully private, AI-enhanced productivity measurement platforms.
